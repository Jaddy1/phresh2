About

Phresh is the first app-based cleaning service for college students, by college students. The business will aid in providing jobs by hiring college students as dorm cleaners and handy people for others on campus. The business model will follow a circular form by helping to alleviate the debts of students and give others the time they need to complete their responsibilities without worrying about chores. Finally, to ensure the mental wellness of others, portions of the company's profits will be used to host monthly mental health workshops on campus.
